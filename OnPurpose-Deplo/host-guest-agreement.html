Host-Guest Agreement

OnPurpose NYC - Mutual expectations for authentic experiences

Agreement Overview

This Host-Guest Agreement establishes mutual expectations, responsibilities, and guidelines for experiences facilitated through OnPurpose. Both parties agree to uphold these standards to ensure safe, respectful, and authentic connections.

Core Principle: OnPurpose facilitates "connection, not dating" - all interactions should be professional, respectful, and focused on authentic cultural exchange and learning.

Host Commitments

Experience Quality

  • Provide authentic, well-planned experiences as described in your profile
  • Arrive prepared with necessary materials, knowledge, and enthusiasm
  • Maintain professional standards throughout the experience
  • Respect agreed-upon time limits and itinerary
  • Ensure guest safety and comfort at all times

Communication & Availability

  • Respond to booking requests within 24 hours
  • Confirm meeting details 24 hours before the experience
  • Provide clear instructions for meeting location and what to bring
  • Be punctual and notify guests immediately of any delays
  • Maintain professional communication through OnPurpose platform

Safety & Professionalism

  • Meet guests in public, well-lit locations initially
  • Maintain current background check and verification status
  • Carry appropriate insurance coverage
  • Report any safety concerns or incidents to OnPurpose immediately
  • Respect guest boundaries and maintain appropriate professional distance

Pre-Experience Host Checklist:

  • ☐ Confirm booking details and meeting location
  • ☐ Prepare materials and plan route/activities
  • ☐ Check weather and adjust plans if necessary
  • ☐ Review guest preferences and any special requests
  • ☐ Ensure phone is charged and emergency contacts available

Guest Commitments

Respect & Engagement

  • Treat hosts with respect, courtesy, and professionalism
  • Engage actively and authentically in the experience
  • Follow host instructions and safety guidelines
  • Respect local customs, venues, and other people encountered
  • Maintain appropriate boundaries and professional interaction

Punctuality & Preparation

  • Arrive on time for scheduled experiences
  • Come prepared with items specified by the host
  • Dress appropriately for the planned activities
  • Notify host immediately if running late or need to cancel
  • Bring necessary identification and payment method

Communication & Feedback

  • Communicate preferences, concerns, or special needs in advance
  • Ask questions and engage in meaningful conversation
  • Provide honest, constructive feedback through the review system
  • Report any issues or inappropriate behavior to OnPurpose
  • Keep communication within the OnPurpose platform

Pre-Experience Guest Checklist:

  • ☐ Review host profile and experience details
  • ☐ Confirm meeting time and location
  • ☐ Prepare questions about the experience or local area
  • ☐ Bring required items and appropriate clothing
  • ☐ Ensure phone is charged and emergency contacts notified

Mutual Responsibilities

Safety First

  • Both parties prioritize personal safety and well-being
  • Trust instincts and speak up about any concerns
  • Meet in public spaces for initial interactions
  • Keep emergency contact information accessible
  • Follow all applicable laws and venue policies

Professional Boundaries

  • Maintain appropriate professional distance
  • No romantic or sexual advances (this is not a dating platform)
  • Respect personal space and privacy
  • Keep interactions focused on the experience and cultural exchange
  • Report inappropriate behavior immediately
Prohibited Behavior: Harassment, discrimination, romantic advances, sharing personal contact information, illegal activities, or attempting to circumvent platform fees will result in immediate account suspension.

Experience Categories & Expectations

Local Expert Experiences

  • Focus on neighborhood knowledge, food recommendations, hidden gems
  • Expect walking tours, local dining, cultural insights
  • Duration: Typically 2-4 hours

Cultural Guide Experiences

  • Emphasis on history, art, music, immigration stories
  • May include museum visits, cultural sites, storytelling
  • Duration: Typically 2-6 hours

Wellness Coach Experiences

  • Focus on meditation, fitness, mindfulness activities
  • May include yoga, walking meditation, wellness workshops
  • Duration: Typically 1-3 hours

Creative Mentor Experiences

  • Hands-on learning in art, photography, writing, design
  • Expect skill-building, creative exercises, portfolio review
  • Duration: Typically 2-4 hours

Professional Networker Experiences

  • Industry insights, career advice, networking opportunities
  • May include coffee meetings, industry events, mentorship
  • Duration: Typically 1-2 hours

Cancellation & Rescheduling

Guest-Initiated Changes

  • 24+ hours notice: Full refund available
  • 12-24 hours notice: 50% refund, host compensation
  • Less than 12 hours: No refund, full host compensation
  • Emergency situations: Case-by-case review

Host-Initiated Changes

  • Hosts should provide as much notice as possible
  • Guests receive full refund for host cancellations
  • Hosts may face penalties for last-minute cancellations
  • Alternative dates should be offered when possible

Dispute Resolution Process

  1. Direct Communication: Attempt to resolve issues directly through respectful dialogue
  2. Platform Mediation: Contact OnPurpose customer service for assistance
  3. Formal Review: OnPurpose investigates and makes binding decisions
  4. Account Actions: Violations may result in warnings, suspensions, or permanent bans

Payment & Compensation

Payment Structure

  • Guests pay upon booking confirmation
  • Hosts receive 80% of booking fee (OnPurpose retains 20%)
  • Payouts processed within 24-48 hours after experience completion
  • All transactions processed securely through Stripe

Additional Expenses

  • Guests responsible for their own transportation, meals, and activity fees
  • Hosts should clearly communicate any additional costs in advance
  • No cash transactions outside the OnPurpose platform

Agreement Acknowledgment

By using OnPurpose and participating in experiences, both hosts and guests acknowledge that they have read, understood, and agree to abide by this Host-Guest Agreement.

This agreement is automatically accepted upon:

  • Host: Completing profile verification and first experience listing
  • Guest: Making first booking through the platform

Violations of this agreement may result in:

  • Account warnings or temporary suspension
  • Permanent ban from the OnPurpose platform
  • Forfeiture of payments or refunds
  • Legal action in cases of serious misconduct
