Privacy Policy

OnPurpose NYC - Your privacy matters to us

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

Data Type Purpose Legal Basis
Name, Email, Phone Account creation and communication Contract performance
Profile Photos Host/guest identification Legitimate interest
Location Data Host discovery and matching Consent

Experience History Service improvement and recommendations Legitimate interest

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device information (IP address, browser type, operating system)
  • Usage data (pages visited, time spent, click patterns)
  • Location data (when permission granted)
  • Cookies and similar tracking technologies

2. How We Use Your Information

Primary Purpose: To facilitate safe, authentic connections between hosts and guests in NYC through our hospitality platform.

2.1 Service Operations

  • Create and manage user accounts
  • Process bookings and payments
  • Facilitate communication between hosts and guests
  • Verify host qualifications and backgrounds
  • Provide customer support

2.2 Safety and Security

  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Conduct background checks on hosts
  • Monitor for inappropriate behavior
  • Respond to safety incidents

2.3 Platform Improvement

  • Analyze usage patterns and preferences
  • Develop new features and services
  • Personalize recommendations
  • Conduct research and analytics

3. Information Sharing

3.1 With Other Users

We share limited profile information between hosts and guests to facilitate bookings:

  • Name and profile photo
  • Public reviews and ratings
  • General location (neighborhood level)
  • Experience descriptions and availability

3.2 With Service Providers

  • Stripe: Payment processing and host payouts
  • SendGrid: Email communications
  • Cloudinary: Image hosting and processing
  • Netlify/Neon: Platform hosting and database services

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and safety.

5. Your Privacy Rights

5.1 GDPR Rights (EU Residents)

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate information
  • Erasure: Delete your personal data
  • Portability: Transfer data to another service
  • Restriction: Limit how we use your data
  • Objection: Opt out of certain data processing

5.2 CCPA Rights (California Residents)

  • Know what personal information we collect
  • Delete personal information we have collected
  • Opt out of the sale of personal information
  • Non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

5.3 How to Exercise Rights

Contact us at privacy@onpurpose.com or through your account settings. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies and Tracking

6.1 Types of Cookies

  • Essential: Required for platform functionality
  • Performance: Help us understand how you use our service
  • Functional: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing: Deliver relevant advertisements (with consent)

6.2 Cookie Management

You can control cookies through your browser settings or our cookie preference center. Note that disabling essential cookies may affect platform functionality.

7. Data Security

7.1 Security Measures

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Encrypted data storage
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Employee training on data protection

7.2 Breach Notification

In the event of a data breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities within 72 hours as required by applicable law.

8. International Data Transfers

Your data may be processed in countries outside your residence. We ensure adequate protection through:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Appropriate safeguards and security measures

9. Children's Privacy

OnPurpose is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such collection, we will delete the information immediately.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. We will notify users of material changes via email or platform notification.
