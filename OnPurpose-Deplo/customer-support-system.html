‚ùì Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel or reschedule a booking?

You can cancel or reschedule through your account dashboard. Cancellation policies vary by timing:

  • 24+ hours: Full refund
  • 12-24 hours: 50% refund
  • Less than 12 hours: No refund
What should I do if my host doesn't show up?

Contact emergency support immediately at emergency@onpurpose.com. You'll receive a full refund and we'll help find an alternative experience if possible.

How do I report inappropriate behavior?

Report immediately through:

  • Emergency support: emergency@onpurpose.com
  • In-app report button
  • Call our emergency line (available 24/7)

We take all reports seriously and investigate within 24 hours.

When will I receive my host payout?

Host payouts are processed within 24-48 hours after experience completion. Payments go directly to your connected bank account via Stripe.

How do I update my profile information?

Log into your account and navigate to Profile Settings. Some changes (like verification documents) may require admin approval.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, debit cards, and digital wallets through our secure Stripe integration. PayPal coming soon.
